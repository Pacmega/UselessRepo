Events

We do many different kinds of things at our school. Teaching is just one of the many things we do for and with our students! A few recent examples of our extracurricular activities are:

  • We organised a robot building contest;
  • We built and launched a rocket that can fly up to 1 km high and
  • Several of our students worked together to organise a christmas dinner for refugees.
And we also do much more! Our students are always welcome to give their own suggestions, and we are always sure to take these ideas seriously and try our best to make them come true.
