Our goal

At Horizon Academy we aim to give children the very best education there is.

How do we do this?

  • By using the best up-to-date developments in teaching strategies
  • Implementing new developments into our curriculum
  • Providing depth in different subjects like politics and technology

We do this not only via classes. To take Politics for example, we have a yearly event where we take our discussions to the county hall!

Our handbook

To make sure everything is clear as possible we have a handbook made openly available. In this rulebook we have specified what is and what isn't allowed, because even though we do allow many things, not everything can be permitted.

With this handbook everyone is provided with a clear image of our policies in regards to appropriate attire, attendance and so on.

Our information system: Vital Information Resource - Universally Serious

There are many kinds of information that should be available to our students. For example information a teacher wishes to provide for their students, or homework assignments for students who missed a class. This data, together with several other kinds of functional information is provided to students and teachers alike via our Vital Information Resource - Universally Serious network.
